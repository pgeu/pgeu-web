Diversity Committee Reports

The PostgreSQL Europe Diversity Committee has committed to providing regular reports to the PostgreSQL Europe board and to the PostgreSQL Europe community.

Reports will be produced annually, at the end of the calendar year. In addition an interim report will be provided et the end of each quarter.

As an exception, the Committee's first annual report covers the 12 month period to the end of March 2025.
