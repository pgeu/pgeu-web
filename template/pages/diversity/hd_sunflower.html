Hidden Disabilities Sunflower

Global Sunflower Network

We are proud to announce that PostgreSQL Europe has joined the global Sunflower network.

We are now an official member of the Hidden Disabilities Sunflower, a globally recognised symbol that supports people living with non-visible disabilities.

Whether or not you have a non-visible disability, this is about creating an environment that is more understanding, more supportive, and more inclusive for everyone.

The Sunflower is a simple way to say: “I may need a little more support, time, space, or understanding today.”

Together, we can create a community where everyone feels seen, valued, and supported.

The Sunflower Pledge

By joining the global Hidden Disabilities Sunflower network, PostgreSQL Europe pledges to create a supportive and inclusive environment for individuals with non-visible disabilities who may need additional assistance, understanding, or more time.

Specifically, to commit to:

  • Training our workforce: Before we launch the Sunflower and continue over time, we will aim to train at least 80% of our current and future staff and volunteers. This training will help them understand what the Sunflower symbol means, what non-visible disabilities are, and how to approach and support individuals wearing the Sunflower.
  • Promoting awareness: We aim to increase awareness of our Sunflower-friendly approach by sharing information about the Sunflower on our website's accessibility page and, when relevant, on the Sunflower location map. We'll also use our communication platforms to educate our team and customers about different non-visible disabilities, conditions, and chronic illnesses, helping to build understanding.
  • Creating an inclusive environment: We aim to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome, and people with non-visible disabilities feel respected, valued, and supported.

Sunflower-Friendly Events

PostgreSQL Conference Europe 2025 is officially Sunflower-friendly. Our organisers and volunteers are completing Sunflower training to better support people with non-visible disabilities.

All other PostgreSQL Europe events are invited and encouraged to become Sunflower-friendly. If you're organising a PostgreSQL Europe event, please contact the diversity committee to learn more and to ask for help with this.
